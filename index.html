About Us

Woody Aura is a wood craft company in Kurunegala, Sri Lanka, creating home items that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design. They're committed to quality, sustainability, and customer service, using eco-friendly materials and processes. The company was founded by a passionate woodworker and offers unique, handmade pieces for home decor.
